Zero Wine Tryouts is a fork project based on Zero Wine (http://sourceforge.net/projects/zerowine/) written by Joxean Koret.

The Zero Wine Tryouts project is maintained by Chae Jong Bin.

Current project members 
	Chae Jong Bin 
	Frank Poz

Zero Wine Tryouts depend on:
	Python (http://www.python.org/)
	Wine (http://www.winehq.org/)
	Xvfb (http://www.x.org/archive/X11R6.8.2/doc/Xvfb.1.html)
	pefile (http://code.google.com/p/pefile/) by Ero Carrera
	python-ptrace (http://bitbucket.org/haypo/python-ptrace/wiki/) by Victor Stinner
	TrID (http://mark0.net/soft-trid-e.html) by Marco Pontello
	UPX (http://upx.sourceforge.net/) by:
		Markus Franz Xaver Johannes Oberhumer
		Laszlo Molnar
	GNU (http://www.gnu.org/) software by Free Software Foundation (http://www.fsf.org/) , Inc.
	TCPDUMP (http://www.tcpdump.org/) by:
		Bill Fenner
		David Young
		Fulvio Risso
		Guy Harris
		Hannes Gredler
		Michael Richardson
	pdftk (http://www.accesspdf.com/pdftk/) by Sid Steward
	pdf-parser (http://blog.didierstevens.com/programs/pdf-tools/) by Didier Stevens
	pdfid (http://blog.didierstevens.com/programs/pdf-tools/) by Didier Stevens

	Keivan Komeilipour (http://www.keivan.ir/) (2010/02/04~)
